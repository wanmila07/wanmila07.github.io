I am a PhD candidate in Artificial Intelligence at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM), specialising in multi-objective evolutionary algorithms with adaptive operators applied to constrained optimisation problems in the informal business sector.

My research addresses the NP-hard problem of incentive distribution among informal SMEs, combining computational intelligence with real-world policy data from the World Bank Indonesian Survey of Informal Sector Enterprises (ISES 2023).

I have experience in machine learning, data mining, full-stack development, and applied analytics across domains including financial modelling, air quality prediction, and IoT security.
