## Overview

A constrained multi-objective optimization system for SME incentive allocation under competing policy objectives — Efficiency, Equity, and Sustainability.

## Problem

Malaysia's informal SME incentive programmes operate on first-come-first-served basis. Policymakers must choose between growth, equity, and sustainability. No current system handles all three simultaneously under real budget constraints.

## Approach

Multi-Objective Evolutionary Algorithm (MOEA) with adaptive operators generates Pareto-optimal allocation sets — showing policymakers real tradeoffs rather than forcing a single answer.
